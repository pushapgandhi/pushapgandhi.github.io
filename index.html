About Me

Hi! I am Pushap (Pushp) Gandhi, a passionate Data Scientist with over three years of hands-on industry experience.

My expertise lies in a comprehensive understanding of mathematical principles underlying Machine Learning and Deep Learning concepts. This is evident from my diverse project portfolio, covering the entire spectrum from data mining and analysis to the deployment of machine learning models.

As a Business Intelligence specialist, I enable organizations to make data-driven decisions and effectively convey insights to new audiences. Starting from conceptualizing measurement goals and ensuring accurate data capture, through data preparation and analysis, I skillfully navigate each stage of the process.
